First Recipe

Black Daal

    Ingredients

  • 1 cup black daal
  • 1 cup vegetable broth
  • 3 cups water
  • 1 tspn cayenne pepper
  • 1 tbspn corriandar powder
  • 1/2 tspn salt
  • 1 tspn cumin seeds
  • 2 cloves minced garlic

    Steps

  1. Wash the daal until water runs clear
  2. Add the water and vegetable broth in a the pot
  3. Add in the daal
  4. Mix in the cayenne pepper, corriandar powder, and salt
  5. add olive oil to cover the small frying pan
  6. add the cumin seeds and minced garlic
  7. wait until the garlic is brown then add to the daal

Second Recipe

Chickpeas

    Ingredients

  • 1 tbspn olive oil
  • 2 cans of chickpeas
  • 1 onion
  • 1 tspn salt
  • 1 tspn cumin seeds
  • 1 tspn kalonji
  • 1/4 tspn turmeric powder
  • 1 tspn of cayenne pepper

    Steps

  • Add olive oil to the pot and fry onions until transparent
  • Add kalonji, cumin seeds, salt, cayenne pepper and tumeric
  • Add chickpeas and enough water to cover the chickpeas
  • Boil the chickpeas and wait until the water is evaporated
